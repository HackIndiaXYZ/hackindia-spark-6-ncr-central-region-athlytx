## 🎤 Q&A Preparation

### Expected Questions:

**Q: How do you ensure data privacy?**
A: Health records are encrypted before uploading to IPFS. Only the athlete has the decryption key. We use Fernet symmetric encryption and plan to implement zero-knowledge proofs for selective disclosure.

**Q: Why Polygon and not Ethereum?**
A: Polygon offers low transaction fees (< ₹1) and fast confirmation times (2-3 seconds), making it ideal for frequent credential updates. We can scale to millions of athletes without high gas costs.

**Q: How accurate is your AI model?**
A: Our current MVP uses a rule-based system. We're training an XGBoost model on 10,000+ athlete data points with 85% accuracy in predicting injury risk within 30 days.

**Q: What if an athlete loses their wallet?**
A: Web3Auth provides social recovery. Athletes can recover their wallet using their email or phone number. We also maintain a backup of encrypted credentials.

**Q: How do you monetize?**
A: We charge sports academies and clubs for bulk verification services, analytics dashboards, and API access. Athletes always use ATHLYX for free.

**Q: What's your go-to-market strategy?**
A: We're partnering with 5 sports academies in Delhi NCR for pilot programs. We'll onboard 1,000 athletes in the first 3 months and expand to other cities based on feedback.

**Q: How do you compete with existing solutions?**
A: Current solutions are centralized, paper-based, or don't use blockchain. ATHLYX is the first to combine Web3, AI, and Indian-specific features like our food database.

**Q: What's your traction so far?**
A: We've built a working MVP, deployed smart contracts on Polygon Mumbai, and have LOIs from 3 sports academies representing 500+ athletes.
